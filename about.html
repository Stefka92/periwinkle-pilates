About

What is Pilates?

Pilates is a form of exercise which concentrates on strengthening the body with an emphasis on core strength. This helps to improve general fitness and overall well-being.Pilates concentrates on posture, balance and flexibility.

What else to expect?

During this three-day retreat you will also embrace in cold water sea swims which has significant health benefits for your immune system as well as improved circulation.
You will practice meditation and mindfulness daily, which will keep your mind calm and focused throughout the day
As part of the package, you will be provided with 2 nights' accommodation in a 4-star hotel just a stone's throw from the beach
and will get daily meals provided as well as cold-pressed juices to detoxify the body.
The total cost of the retreat is $499 which requires a security deposit of 150 paid 4 weeks in advance .
